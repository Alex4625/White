White Day is celebrated one month after Valentine's Day.

The tradition started in Japan, where people give return gifts to someone who gave them chocolates on Valentine's Day.

There is also a concept called "sanbai gaeshi", which means the return gift should be three times more special.

But honestly, the best gift is simply reminding someone how much they mean to you.
